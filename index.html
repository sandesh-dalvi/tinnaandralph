Located on the southwest coast of India, Goa is famous for its idyllic warm weather on the coasts of the Arabian Sea. January may be the coolest month of the year in Goa, but the region still enjoys beautiful temperatures making it a perfect time of year for weddings in the sun.

The average daytime temperature in Goa hovers around 25oC in January, with highs of 31oC during the hottest part of the day. At night the temperature dips to around 20oC. So please plan your wardrobe accordingly.

Here's a ready reckoner to plan your travels to Ralph & Tinna's wedding in Goa, India.

Goa is well connected by air, rail & road connectivity. The nearest airport to the Mercure Devaaya Resort, Divar, Goa is Dabolim International Airport ( South Goa ) which is approximately 37kms & Manohar Parrikar International Airport ( North Goa ) which is approximately 39 kms. The nearest rail head is at Karmali Railway Station at a distance of approximately 12 kms.

You will also need to cross over the river Mandovi to reach Divar islands, via a ferry service which is operational from 6am to 12pm. The nearest ferry point is the Old Goa Ferry Terminal near Viceroy's Arch, Old Goa.
